2013 基督之家聯合退修會


The Home of Christ Church Joint Retreat Conference, 2013

As Christians we are all on a journey to Christ-likeness (Romans 8:29). Our goal for this retreat is for each participant to take one more step in the discipleship process. Recognizing that we are all on a journey and each at a different point in the discipleship process, we all have something to offer one another-encouragement, insight, exhortation, and warning.
The Journey: One Step Closer
 
Guest Speaker: Pastor Scott Hunnicutt
